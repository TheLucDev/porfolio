About Me

Hi, I'm The Luc, a dedicated and enthusiastic Full Stack Developer with > 1.5 years of professional experience in crafting dynamic and responsive web applications. My journey in web development has been driven by a passion for technology and a commitment to continuous learning and improvement.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, where I gained a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end development. My skill set includes proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks like React and NextJS , VueJs on the front-end, as well as Node.js, Express.js, and NestJs on the back-end. I am also experienced in working with databases such as MySQL, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL.

During my career, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, ranging from small business websites to complex web applications. I've developed a keen eye for detail and a strong ability to solve problems efficiently. One of my key strengths is my adaptability, allowing me to quickly learn and implement new technologies and methodologies to meet project requirements.

I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy working closely with cross-functional teams to bring innovative ideas to life. My experience includes implementing user authentication, creating RESTful APIs, optimizing application performance, and ensuring security best practices. I am also well-versed in version control using Git and continuous integration/deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.

Beyond my technical skills, I am a proactive communicator and a strong advocate for clean, maintainable code. I believe in the power of code reviews and pair programming to foster knowledge sharing and improve code quality. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer and contribute to impactful projects.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the latest tech trends, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying outdoor activities. I am excited about the future of web development and look forward to being a part of innovative and transformative projects.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work.
